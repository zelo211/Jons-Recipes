Chicken Alfredo

Description

When it comes to indulgent comfort foods, I dont know if anything beats Chicken Alfredo. I just cant say no to a big ol plate of carby pasta drenched in an ultra-rich and creamy sauce and topped with juicy strips of seared chicken, can you? And now that I know how easy it is to make it at home, I have a feeling this is going to become my new favorite special occasion meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken breasts
  • Pasta
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of butter
  • 1/3 Tablespoon of Pepper
  • 1/3 Tablespoon of salt
  • 1 32 fl oz of Chicken Broth
  • 1 1/2 Cups of Heavy cream
  • 1/2 Cup of Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Chop the garlic and chicken breasts
  2. Put Oil and Garlic in the Pan
  3. Add Chicken to the Pan
  4. Add Butter, Salt, and Pepper
  5. Add Chicken Broth
  6. Add Your Pasta
  7. Let it Soften
  8. Add Cream, Butter, and Cheese
  9. And Now You Are Done!
