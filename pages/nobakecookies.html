No Bake Cookies

Description

Delicious no-bake chocolate oatmeal cookies with peanut butter and cocoa flavor â€” all without turning on the oven.

Ingridients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2cup margarine
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 cups quick cooking oats
  • 1/2 cup peanutbutter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Bring sugar, margarine, milk, cocoa, and salt to a rapid boil in a saucepan for 1 minute.
  2. Add quick-cooking oats, peanut butter, and vanilla; mix well.
  3. Working quickly, drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper and let cool
  4. All Done!
